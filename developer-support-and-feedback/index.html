Developer Support and Feedback

Developer Forum

The GenomeSpace tool developer discussion group provides a forum where tool developers who wish to add their tools to GenomeSpace can ask questions, provide feedback and comments, and request new features.  The GenomeSpace team also uses this forum for announcements and news regarding the early tool adopter program. Note that there is also a separate discussion group for GenomeSpace users, if you have more general questions and feedback about GenomeSpace. Sign up for the forums on the Support page.

Reporting Bugs

The GenomeSpace team appreciates your feedback. GenomeSpace is still in development, and bug reports will help improve the quality and usability of the software.  Please report bugs to gs-help@broadinstitute.org.
